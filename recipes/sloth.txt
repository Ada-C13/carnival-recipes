Chris' Steamed Buns
-------------------

Servings: 24

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1. Mix together yeast, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 cup flour, and 1/4 cup warm water. 
Allow to stand for 30 minutes.

6. Divide dough into 2 parts, and place the piece you are not working with in a covered bowl. Divide each half into 12 parts.

9. Place steam-plate on a small wire rack in the middle of the wok.
11. Cover wok with lid and steam buns over boiling water for 15 minutes.
